About VITA LAB

Understand the unseen. Inspire the future.

VITA LAB pioneers breakthrough research in affective computing and multimodal AI through rigorous mathematical frameworks. We aim to decode human emotions and behaviors to empower educators and learners alike. Our ultimate goal is to bridge complex neural architectures with human-centric values, fostering a joyful and profoundly connected educational ecosystem.
